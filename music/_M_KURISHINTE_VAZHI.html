1- Station

Maranathinaaividhichu Karayatta Daivathin Kunjaadine
Aparadhiyayi vidhichu Kalmasham Kalaraatha Karthavine

Ariyatha Kuttaghal Nirayayi Chumathi Parishudhannaya Ninnil
Kaivalya Datha Nin, Kaarunyamkaikondor Kadanathillazhthi Ninne
Avasaanavidhiyil Nee Alivaarnnu Njaghalkkayi Aruleename Naagabhaaghyam

2- Station

Kurishuchumanneedunnu Lokathin Vinakal chumannedunnu
Neegunnu Divyanaadhan Ninnanam Nirayum Nirathilude

En Janame Cholka Njan Enthu Chythu Kurishente Tholilettan
Poonthen thulumbhunna Nattil Njan Nighale Aasshayodaanayichu
Endeyidam Ningal Ellam Marannente Athmaavinathangametti

3- Station

Kurishin Kanath Bhaaram thanguvan Kazhiyathe Lokanaathan
Paadangal Padariveenu Kallukal Nirayum Peruvazhiyil

Thripaadam Kallinmel Thattimurinju Chenninam Vaarnnozhuki
Maanavarilla, Vaanavarilla Thangithunacheeduvan
Anuthapamoorunna Chudukannuneerthooki Anayunnu Munnil Njaghal

4- Station

Vazhiyil Karanju Vannorammaye Thanayan Thirinju Nokki
Swargeeya Kanthichinthum Mizhikalil Koorambu Thaaniragi

Aarodu Ninne Njan Samyapeduthum Kadhanaperum Kadale
Aararinjazhathilala Thalli Nilkunna Ninmano Veedhana
Nin Kannuneeral Kazhukenamennil Pathiyunna Malinyamellam

5- Station

Kurishu Chumannu Neeghum Naadhane Shimayon Thunacheedunnu
Naadha Nin Kurishu Thaaghan Kaivanna Bhaagyame Bhaagyam

Ninkurishetrayo Lolam Nin Nukam Aanandha Dhaayakam
Azhalil Veenuzhalunnorkkavalambhamekunna Kurishe Namichedunnu
Sura Loka Naadha Nin Kurishonnu Thaanguvan Tarane Varangal Nirantham

6- Station

Vaadithalarnnu Mugham, Naadhante Kannukal Thaanu Manghi
Veeronica Mizhineer Thookiya Divyananam Thudachu

Malaghamaarkellam Anandhamekunna Maanathe Poonilave
Thaabormaamal Mele Nin Mugham Sooryane Pole Minni
Inna Mugathinte Laavanyamonnake Manghi Dukhathil Mungi

7- Station

Uchaveyilil porinju Dussaha Mardhanathal Valanju
Dheeham Thalarnnu Thanu Rakshakan Veendum Nilathu Veenu

Lokappapanghalananghaye Veezhichu Veedanippichathevam
Bhaaram Niranjora Krushu Nirmichathen Paapaghal Thanneyallo
Thaapam Kalarnnaghe, Paadam Punarnnu Njan Keezhunnu Kaniyenamennil

8- Station

Orslemin Puthrimare Nighalinnenneyorthenthineevam
Karayunnu Nighaleeyum Suthareeyum Orthorthu Kenu Kolvin

Veedhan Thingunna Kaalam Varunnu Kanneraninja Kaalam
Malakale Njaghale Mooduvin Vegamennaravam Kelkkumennum
Karal Nonthu Karayunna Naariganathinu Naathan Samashwasameki

9- Station

Kaikalukal Kuzhanju Nathante Thirumayi Thalarnnulanju
Kurishumayi Moonnamathum Poozhiyil Veezhunnu Daiva Puthran

Mezhukupolennude Hrudayamuruki Kandam Varandunaghi
Taanupoyi Naaventte Deeham Nurungi Maranam Paranniraghi
Valarunnu Dukhaghal Thalarunnu Poomeni Urukunnu Karalintte Ullam

10- Station

Ethi Vilaapayathra Kaalvari Kunnin Mukal Padarppil
Naadhante Vastramellam Shatrukkal Onnayurinju Neekki

Vairikal Thingi Varunentte Chuttilum Khooramam Gadgadaghal
Bhaagicheduthentte Vastraghalellam Paapikal Vairikal
Naadha Vishudhi Than Thoovella Vashthragal Kanivaarnnu Chaarthenamenne

11- Station

Kurishil Kidatheedunnu Naadhante Kai Kal Tharacheedunnu
Marthyanu Raksha Nalkan Ethiya Divyamam Kaikaalukal

Kanivatta Vairikal Cheernnu thulachentte Kaikalum Kaalukalum
Perukunnu Veedhana Urukunnu Chethana Nilayatta Neerkkayam
Maranam Parathiyorirulil Kudughi Njan Bhayamenne Onnayi Vizhughi

12- Station

Kurishil Kidannu Jeevan Vediyunnu Bhuvanika Naadhaneesho
Sooryan Maranjirundu Naadeghum Andhakaaram Niranju

Narikalkkuraghuvan Alayundu Paravaykku Koodunttu Paarkkuvan
Naraputhranoozhiyil Thalayonnu chaaykkuvan Edamilloreedavum
Pulkoodu thottaghe Pulkunna daaridryam Kurisholam Koottayi Vannu

13- Station

Arumasuthante Meni Mathavu Madiyil Kidatheedunnu
Alayaazhi Pole Naadhe Nin Dukham Athiru Kaanathathallo

Perukunna Santhaapa Munayeetta ho Ninte Hridayam Pilarnnuvallo
Aararumilla thellaswaasamekuvan Aakula Naayike
Muttunna DUkhathil Chuttum Thiranju Njan Kittiloraswasamegum

14- Station

Naadhante Divya Ddeham Vidhipole Samskaricheedunnitha
