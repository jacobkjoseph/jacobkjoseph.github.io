Precious Spirit come, Holy Spirit come
Come into me now to touch and heal
Precious Spirit come, Holy Spirit come,
Come into me now and set me free.

Oh let my heart burn with love, with love for my God, 
Let thy mighty power flow and set me free.

When times of trial come, temptations come my way
Let me cling to thy precious love
Prepare me for the day, that great and glorious day
When my Lord comes back to take me home.
